Fun Facts:

-This type of duck is considered one of the largest ducks

-This type of duck is one of the agile and fastest duck.

-When the ducks first hatch they have dusty yellow heads which then turn to brown. Overtime they become speckled with black and white on their heads.

-They are active at night.

-They make loud sounds to keep predators away. Supposeldy they sound like ghosts.
